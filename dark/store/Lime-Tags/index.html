What are Lime Tags?


Lime Tags are tiny electronic tags that once tapped by your phone, can give you information like your accounts, or how to contact you if your valuble things gets stolen.



How does Lime Tags work?


No setup or extra apps are needed, as long as you have a phone with NFC, you can just put the top edge of your phone near the tag and it will show you a link to the Lime Network.



What is the Lime Network?


The Lime Network is a service that powers Lime Tags. The Lime Network can show your accounts, give people your contact information, and more!



How to order Lime Tags?


Once you pay using a service provider (e.g Cash App) you will get contacted using your phone number in the description. We'll handle the rest from there.
